ABOUT US

Clever Groups is a preference-based group generator. This means that each student in a class can submit a list of preferences of who they want to be in a group with, from most to least. The teacher can then generate groups by selecting the max size of the groups or the total number of groups.

This is a democratic alternative to the traditional ways of creating groups. The groups are created by an unbiased deterministic algorithm (includes no randomness). The group making algorithm seeks to maximize overall satisfaction based on the preferences submitted by the students. When creating random groups there is a risk of creating dysfunctional groups, that is pairing people that cannot work together.
